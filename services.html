Our Services

  • Check-Up Visits: Regular comprehensive check-ups for children of all ages. These visits include physical exams, growth and development tracking, immunization reviews, and anticipatory guidance for parents.
  • Sick Visits: Same-day appointments are available for sudden illnesses such as fever, colds, flu, ear infections, sore throat, or other urgent pediatric concerns.
  • Sleep disturbance Visits: We offer comprehensive consultations for sleep-related issues in both children and adults, including conditions such as sleep apnea, insomnia, sleepwalking, restless leg syndrome, bedwetting, and more. Our team also provides ongoing CPAP monitoring and management for patients with sleep apnea. When necessary, we collaborate closely with specialists to coordinate surgical interventions such as tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy, and Inspire implant therapy.
  • Sleep Lab: We have inhouse state of art Sleep lab and quick appointments for conducting sleep studies.
  • Behavioral/ADHD Consultations: Expertise in managing autism, ADHD, and school-related issues.
  • Sports/Camp Physicals: Pre-participation physicals for school or camp activities.
