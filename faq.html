What are the signs of a sleep disorder?
Symptoms may include excessive daytime sleepiness, loud snoring, gasping for air during sleep, difficulty falling or staying asleep, and waking up unrefreshed.
How is a sleep study conducted?
Sleep studies can be performed in a sleep lab or at home using specialized equipment to monitor brain activity, breathing, heart rate, and movement.
What treatments are available for sleep apnea?
Treatment options include CPAP therapy, oral appliances, lifestyle modifications, and in some cases, surgery.
Do children suffer from sleep disorders?
Yes, children can experience sleep disorders such as sleep apnea, insomnia, night terrors, and restless leg syndrome.

When will you give me results of my sleep study?
We can perform sleep studies within a week or sometimes in days. We strive to provide results of the studies within 48 hours of completing the study.
