What are the signs of a sleep disorder?
Symptoms may include excessive daytime sleepiness, loud snoring, gasping for air during sleep, difficulty falling or staying asleep, and waking up unrefreshed.
How is a sleep study conducted?
Sleep studies can be performed in a sleep lab or at home using specialized equipment to monitor brain activity, breathing, heart rate, and movement.
What treatments are available for sleep apnea?
Treatment options include CPAP therapy, oral appliances, lifestyle modifications, and in some cases, surgery.
Do children suffer from sleep disorders?
Yes, children can experience sleep disorders such as sleep apnea, insomnia, night terrors, and restless leg syndrome.
How do I schedule an appointment?
You can schedule a consultation by calling our office or from the homepage of this website.
How do I schedule a Sleep Study?
You can call us to verify insurance and sleep study spots that are available. We like to schedule an in person consultation prior to scheduling a sleep study. You will be shown a sleep study room during the visit and we will explain the procedure and what to expect to you so that you can plan accordingly. We require 150 dollar deposit for scheduling a sleep study. .
Is my sleep study deposit refundable?
The sleep study fee is refundable if the appointment is canceled at least 72 hours prior to the scheduled study. Once the study is scheduled, our technicians are reserved specifically for your appointment and must be paid even if you are unable to attend. Therefore, cancellations made within 72 hours of the appointment or if patients cannot keep their appointment for any reason including if they are sick, out of town or any other reason these fees are non-refundable.
How soon are appointments available?
We are a new practice, and we have appointments available within a week. For urgent matters, we can see patients the same day or next day.
When will you give me results of my sleep study?
We can perform sleep studies within a week or sometimes in days. We strive to provide results of the studies within 48 hours of completing the study.
