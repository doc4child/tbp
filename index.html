👶 Personalized Pediatric Care:

We understand that every child is unique, and we take the time to listen to your concerns and provide personalized care.

Our clinic is designed with your family in mind, offering a welcoming environment where children feel safe and comfortable. We also provide a range of services to support your child’s health, including vaccinations, ear piercing, circumcision, and more.


🌙 Advanced Sleep Medicine Services for All Ages:

  • Board-Certified Sleep Medicine Experts – For children, teens, and adults-all ages
  • Sleep Studies (Polysomnography) – In-lab & at-home tests for snoring, apnea, insomnia, and more
  • Insomnia Treatment – CBT-I, medication management, and holistic care
  • Sleep Apnea Management – CPAP, oral devices, and referrals for surgery if needed
  • Restless Legs Syndrome (RLS) – Diagnosis & care for Restless Leg Syndrome (RLS) and Pediodic Limb Movement Disorder (PLMD)
  • Narcolepsy & Hypersomnia – Treatment for excessive sleepiness & disrupted sleep cycles
  • Pediatric Sleep Disorders – Help for bedtime struggles, frequent waking, or breathing issues, especially for kids with:
    • Pierre Robin Syndrome
    • Down Syndrome
    • Other craniofacial or genetic concerns
